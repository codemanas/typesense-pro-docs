# Welcome to Your Pro Search Features

## Requirements
* As the plugin leverages Typesense features – you will need a minimum of version 0.30
* For typesense Cluster – please visit your cluster configuration
* If you are self-hosting, please visit [https://typesense.org/downloads/](https://typesense.org/downloads/)

### Quick Overview of What You Can Do
*   **Manage Your Content:** Choose which sections of your website (pages, products, posts) can be searched.
*   **Tailor Search Fields:** Tell the system exactly what details—like prices or custom tags—to look at.
*   **Set Up Smart Words:** Teach your search bar to recognize that words like "couch" and "sofa" mean the same thing.
*   **Review Search Analytics:** See popular terms your customers type and catch where they hit "no results" dead ends.
*   **Hand-Pick Results:** Explicitly pin your favorite pages or products right to the top for specific keywords.
