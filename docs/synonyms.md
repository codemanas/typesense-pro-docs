# Creating Smart Words (Synonyms)

People describe things differently. If someone searches your site for a *"phone"*, you don't want them missing out on great results just because you typed *"iphone"* or *"smartphone"* in your post. Smart Words bridge that gap.

### Setting Up a New Word List
1. Click **Create Set** on the left side panel and name your group (e.g., `tech` or `furniture`).
2. Choose your **Synonym Type**:
    *   **Multi-way synonym (Recommended):** Makes all listed words completely interchangeable. Searching for *any* word in the list finds *all* of them (e.g., typing "couch", "sofa", or "loveseat" yields the same results).
    *   **One-way synonym:** Directs a specific word to a broader target, but not vice-versa.
3. Type your words into the **Synonyms** box, separated clearly with a comma.
4. Click **Add Item**. Your new rules are live immediately!
